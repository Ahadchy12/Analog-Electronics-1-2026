## Question 8  
**Define electricity and electronics. Highlight the key differences between the two terms.**

Electricity is the branch of engineering that deals with the generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electrical power. It mainly focuses on high-power systems such as power plants, transmission lines, motors, and household electrical wiring.

Electronics is the branch of engineering that deals with the flow and control of electrons in electronic components such as diodes, transistors, and integrated circuits. It mainly focuses on low-power systems used for signal processing, communication, and control.

**Key Differences:**
- Electricity mainly deals with power generation and usage.
- Electronics mainly deals with signal control and processing.
- Electrical systems usually operate at high voltage and current.
- Electronic systems usually operate at low voltage and current.

## Question 9  
**Choose a specific electronic device or system and explain how electronics contribute to its functionality. Highlight the role of electronic components.**

A smartphone is an example of an electronic device that heavily relies on electronics. Inside a smartphone, electronic components such as transistors, integrated circuits, microprocessors, sensors, and memory chips work together to perform various functions.

The processor controls all operations, transistors act as electronic switches, sensors detect physical inputs such as touch and light, and integrated circuits manage communication, display, and data processing. Without electronics, modern smartphones would not be possible.

## Question 10  
**Explain the scope of electricity and electronics, providing examples of applications for each.**

The scope of electricity includes power generation, transmission, and distribution systems. It is widely used in power stations, electric motors, household wiring, lighting systems, and industrial machinery.

The scope of electronics includes communication systems, medical equipment, computers, automation, robotics, and IoT devices. Electronics play a major role in modern technology such as mobile phones, computers, smart devices, and control systems.
